Tulip Corp.

Tulip Corp. is a web development company created by Patrick Cuauro for the purpose of demonstrating that magic can be created with the right tools and a little bit of imagination. Also after find out our accountant to be a Butterbeer addict that spent the money of the treasury in a brand new wand because misplaced his at the local bar, with his friend the one with he plays Quidditch every Tuesday afternoon at Hogwarts, he decided it was a good idea to make a huge loan with a lender with a very high interest rate, because he though higher interest meant somehow better than low, leaving us with a huge debt for years, we realized we were not the only ones with financial problem, and also Butterbeer addiction! So, we begin a journey to discover enigmatic artifacts around the world to help wizards and charging them as low as possible, because we are not filthy capitalists! But we love Butterbeer!!

Some adventures helped us to practice our spells to defend ourselves from hunger, rain, and mystic weird creatures. We finally found the formula to use to amortize a loan based on the interest, the time, and the initial amount of Butterbeer, I mean money! All the adventures left us with a lesson to learn besides learning spells and avoiding Butterbeer! We also must learn how to be financially responsible, helping others to realize contracts can be broken with forget spells, learn from a famous wizard and a man with obsessions with spiders, that we definitely not used with our loaner, of course. And to conclude, we decided to make the loan app calculator public and free to help others to measure their unfortunate financial crisis solutions.
